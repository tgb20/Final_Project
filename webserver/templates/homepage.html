About:

This project looks at the relationships between 3 podcasts created by Rooster Teeth. It looks at who is on the cast as well as who else was with them. While it does not have a lot of use besides reference for the podcasts, it demonstrates the power of a graph database when it comes to complex relationships.

  • Rooster Teeth Podcast Colored Red
  • Always Open Podcast Colored Yellow
  • The Patch Podcast Colored Blue
